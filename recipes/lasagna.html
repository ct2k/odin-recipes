Super Deluxe Lasagna

Description

The recipe of all recipes! Here you will learn to make Super Deluxe Lasagna.

Ingredients:

  • 6 thick-cut lasagna noodles
  • 2 pounds of ground beef
  • 4 pounds of mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 8 strips of bacon
  • 4 eggs, beaten
  • 500 grams of Smoked Apple Wood Cheddar, shredded
  • 1 red onion, diced
  • tomato sauce
  • salt and pepper
  • olive oil

Directions:

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F
  2. Pour 3 tablespoons olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat
  3. In a large bowl, mix onions in with ground beef
  4. Add salt and pepper to ground beef. Stir well.
  5. Add beef mixture to skillet. Cook until brown.
  6. Boil noodles in large pot - Do not overcook!
  7. Strain noodles
  8. Added first layer of noodles to deep baking dish
  9. Spread tomato sauce, drizzle beaten eggs, cheese, and layer bacon.
  10. Repeat until you are out of noodles / ingredients.
  11. Cook in the oven for 1 hour, no less.
