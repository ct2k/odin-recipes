Pepper Mayo Sandwich

Description:

The secret recipe is finally revealed! You will learn to make the best sandwich in existence.

Ingredients:

  • Sourdough bread, sliced thick.
  • Mayonnaise. Mayonnaise alternatives / substitues are not acceptable.
  • Fresh cracked peppercorns

Directions:

  1. Spread a generous amount of mayo on each slice of your sourdough bread.
  2. Grind fresh peppercorns on to mayo, covering exactly 63% surface area.
  3. Put the two pieces of bread together, forming the sandwich

If some mayo oozes out the side of the sandwich and you can smell the fresh pepper, you have succeeded!
